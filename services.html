Our Services

Comprehensive electro-mechanical solutions for water and energy requirements

Water Pumps

We provide comprehensive water pump solutions for domestic, commercial, and industrial applications. Our services include supply, installation, upgrades, and system enhancements.

  • Surface pumps/booster sets
  • Submersible pumps
  • Dewatering pumps
  • Circulation pumps
  • Pump control systems

We analyze requirements and deliver optimal solutions for effective implementation.

Solar Pumping Systems

We source and install reliable solar-powered systems, enabling efficient energy usage for water pumping and domestic power needs. Our offerings extend to both local and international markets.

  • Solar panels
  • Charge controllers
  • Solar batteries
  • Inverters

Customized solar solutions for sustainable energy independence.

Water Treatment Systems

We deliver complete water treatment solutions for domestic, commercial, and industrial setups, ensuring quality water supply that meets client specifications.

  • Reverse osmosis and ultrafiltration
  • Water softening and disinfection
  • Sediment removal and water testing

Comprehensive water purification and treatment for all requirements.

Firefighting Systems

Our comprehensive services include supply, installation, and maintenance of advanced firefighting systems tailored to client needs.

  • Fire pumps, extinguishers, and sprinklers
  • Hydrants and hoses
  • Fire suppression systems

Complete fire safety solutions for buildings and industrial facilities.

HVAC Systems

We specialize in designing and installing HVAC systems that ensure optimal temperature, humidity, and air quality control for various applications.

  • Air ducts and handling units
  • Blowers, filters, and compressors
  • Complete HVAC system design and installation

Energy-efficient climate control solutions for comfort and industrial processes.
